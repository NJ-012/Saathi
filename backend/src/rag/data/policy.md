# Rangrez Returns & Exchange Policy

## Returns
- Returns are accepted within **7 days** of delivery for unworn, unwashed items with tags attached.
- Sarees, dupattas, and stitched kurtas are eligible for return unless marked "Final Sale".
- Accessories (earrings, necklaces, chokers) are **not eligible for return** once the package is opened, for hygiene reasons.

## Exchanges
- Size exchanges on kurtas are free within 7 days of delivery, subject to stock availability.
- Only one exchange is allowed per order.

## Refunds
- Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within **5–7 business days** of the returned item reaching our warehouse.
- For prepaid orders, the product amount is refunded; original shipping charges are non-refundable.

## Damaged or Wrong Item
- If you received a damaged or incorrect item, contact us within **48 hours of delivery** with photos.
- We arrange a free replacement or full refund in these cases — no return shipping cost to you.

## How to Start a Return or Exchange
1. Share your order ID with us.
2. Our team confirms eligibility and shares pickup details.
3. Once the item is picked up and inspected, the refund or exchange is initiated.
